About Magic City Yachts

Founded in 2015 by passionate maritime enthusiasts, Magic City Yachts began with a vision to transform Miami's luxury yacht charter experience. What started as a boutique operation with two vessels has grown into Miami's premier yacht charter service, thanks to our unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to deliver unforgettable maritime experiences while maintaining the highest standards of safety, luxury, and environmental responsibility. We pride ourselves on our meticulously maintained fleet, professional crew, and personalized service that ensures every voyage exceeds expectations.
